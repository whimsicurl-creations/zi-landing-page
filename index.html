The role

What is an AI Developer?

You've probably already used something an AI Developer built — a chatbot that actually helped you, a tool that summarized a document in seconds, an app that felt like it read your mind. AI Developers are the people who create those experiences. They combine web development skills with AI tools to build apps that are smarter, faster, and more useful than anything that came before.

Companies like Stripe, Duolingo, Morgan Stanley, and Bing have already transformed how they serve their customers using exactly these kinds of tools — and they're just the beginning. It's the fastest-growing skill in tech right now, and the demand for people who can build these apps is only going up.
